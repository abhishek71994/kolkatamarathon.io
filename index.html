IEM-UEM KOLKATA MARATHON 2018

"Run For Charity"

Institute of Engineering and Management, Kolkata(IEM) & University of Engineering and Management, Kolkata(UEM) would like to take this opportunity to introduce the unique and splendid initiative of "Run for Charity" to all students and professors.This truly can turn out to be a significant step towards providing a social message. Its motto is "Freedom Through Education". The IEM-UEM Marathon would be extremely useful and will play an instrumental role in enhancing fitness, wellness, physical awareness along-with enhancement of mental and physical strength. With CRY on our side, all the proceeds which will be amassed from this event will be donated to various NGO associations for Empowering Women Education and Women Involvement in Society.

ALL the profits made from the event will be donated to C.R.Y (Child Rights and You) for the Empowerment of Women Education.
